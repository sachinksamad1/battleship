# Quickstart Guide

This guide will get you into your first match of Battleship in under 2 minutes.

## 1. Choose Your Mode

Upon launching the game, you will be presented with two primary options:
- **Single Player**: Play against the computer AI.
- **Multiplayer**: Create or join a room to play against a friend.

## 2. Ship Placement Phase

Once you enter a match, you must place your fleet:
1. **Drag and Drop**: Pick up ships from your dock and place them on your 10x10 grid.
2. **Rotate**: Double-click or use the 'R' key while dragging to rotate a ship between horizontal and vertical.
3. **Lock In**: Once all 5 ships are placed, click the **Ready** button.

## 3. Starting the Battle

### Single Player
- The AI will automatically place its ships.
- The battle begins as soon as you click **Ready**.

### Multiplayer
- You will receive a **Room Code** (e.g., `BATTLE-X1Y2`).
- Share this code with your opponent.
- The match starts once both players have clicked **Ready**.

## 4. Attacking

- **Your Turn**: Click any cell on the **Enemy Board** to fire a shot.
- **Hit (X)**: If you hit a ship, the cell turns red and you may see an explosion animation.
- **Miss (O)**: If you miss, a blue splash indicator will appear.
- **Sunk**: When all parts of an enemy ship are hit, it will be revealed and marked as sunk.

## 5. Winning

Sink all 5 of your opponent's ships to win the game! You can then choose to start a **Rematch** or return to the **Main Menu**.
