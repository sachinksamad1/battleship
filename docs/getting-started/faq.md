# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General

### Is this game free to play?
Yes! This is an open-source project and is free to play in your browser.

### Do I need to create an account?
No. All multiplayer rooms are temporary, and your settings are saved locally in your browser.

---

## Gameplay

### Can I place ships diagonally?
No. Following the classic rules, ships must be placed horizontally or vertically.

### Can ships touch each other?
Yes. Ships can be adjacent to one another, but they cannot overlap on the same cell.

### How many ships do I have?
You have a total of 5 ships: 1 Carrier (5), 1 Battleship (4), 1 Cruiser (3), 1 Submarine (3), and 1 Destroyer (2).

---

## Technical

### Which browsers are supported?
The game supports all modern evergreen browsers, including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

### I lost my connection during a match. Can I resume?
Yes! Simply refresh the page or re-open the link. The server-authoritative engine will attempt to restore your game state automatically.

### Why is there a delay when I click a cell in multiplayer?
Since the game is server-authoritative, each attack must be validated by the server before the result is shown. Your latency (ping) to the server may cause a slight delay.
